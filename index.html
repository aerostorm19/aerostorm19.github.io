About Me

I’m Abhijit Rai, an Embedded Systems & FPGA Engineer with a strong focus on hardware-software co-design, SDR, IoT communication, and system security. My work bridges the gap between digital logic design and intelligent software, delivering high-performance and energy-efficient systems.

I thrive on building low-level systems that push hardware limits, crafting secure boot flows, FPGA accelerators, and SoC prototypes. At the same time, I’ve explored AI/ML for embedded platforms, blockchain security, and full-stack apps, making me a versatile engineer who can design across layers.

Beyond projects, I’ve mentored students, organized large-scale hackathons, and contributed to open-source. My mission? To engineer systems that are efficient, scalable, and secure while solving real-world challenges.
